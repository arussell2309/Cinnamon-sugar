About Us – Cinnamon Sugar Bakery

History of the business

Cinnamon Sugar was started by our founder and head baker Ahmari Russell. Her passion for creation of delicious treats had blossomed at a young age and she knew she wanted to put a smile on the face of many.

Why Choose Cinnamon Sugar?

Customers should choose Cinnamon Sugar because we offer freshly baked goods made daily with quality ingredients and great attention to taste. Our bakery provides a warm, friendly atmosphere and consistent products that customers can trust. With a wide variety of treats and a focus on customer satisfaction, Cinnamon Sugar delivers both quality and value in every bite.

MISSION & VISION

Mission Statement

To provide freshly baked, high-quality pastries made with care and attention to detail, while creating a warm and welcoming experience for every customer.

Vision Statement

To become a trusted and loved bakery known for delicious treats, excellent service, and bringing comfort and happiness to the community.
